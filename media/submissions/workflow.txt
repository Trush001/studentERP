student ERP System
-------------------------------------------
design and develop 2different apps for this project!
	1) student
	2) faculty
	3) admin
------------------------------------------------
1) student side
- login page with email verification(one time)
-profile page where students can enter their personal detail	
	- there will be some course related details which will be handled and created by the faculty which cannot be changed by the student
- attendance
- course resources 
- results of the exams 
- overall grade and remarks from the faculty 

2) faculty side
- login with email verification(one time)
- profile page where students can enter their personal detail
	-
- leave request page
- student add and manage page for their particular class
- resources page where they can see and upload their personal notes as well
- student exam list where they can give marks based on their work
- attendance page for the faculty
- calendar with the list of the lectures based on time

3) admin
-  login with email verification(one time)
-  profile page where students can enter their personal detail
- admin will handle two different pages for handling staff and student 
	- enrolling student in their respected course
	- enrolling faculty in their field
- handling request of leave 
- managing the attendance of the staff
